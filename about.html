Our Story

Smokey’s Smoked Chicken and Beef was born out of a passion for authentic flavor and sustainable living. We believe that great food starts with great ingredients, fresh, organic, and locally sourced whenever possible.

Mission Statement

To provide fresh, organic produce and sustainable products to foster a healthier lifestyle among our community members.

Vision Statement

To be the leading organic marketplace in the region, promoting sustainability and wellness.

Our Values

  • Sustainability
  • Community Engagement
  • Authentic Flavor
  • Transparency & Trust
